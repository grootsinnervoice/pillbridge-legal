# Privacy Policy for PillBridge

**Last Updated: April 20, 2026**

Welcome to PillBridge. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our app, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've done our best to write this in plain language — if anything is unclear, reach out and we'll answer honestly.

PillBridge is a medication tracking app that helps you manage your medications and, if you choose, lets trusted caregivers monitor your adherence. It's built by a small team, and we don't sell ads, sell data, or profit from anything beyond helping people take their meds on time.

---

## 1. What We Collect

When you use PillBridge, we collect the following types of information:

### Account Information
- Your email address
- Your full name
- A phone number (optional, only if you add one)
- Your password (handled entirely by Firebase Authentication — we never see or store it in plain text)
- Whether your account is a Patient or a Caregiver

### Medication Information
- Medication names, dosages, schedules, and reminder times
- Pill counts and refill history (when refill tracking is enabled)
- Photos of medications or bottles that you choose to add
- A record of doses you've marked as taken, including timestamps
- Titration schedules and phase progression (for dose ramp-ups)

### Care Group Information
- Unique six-character care codes linking patients and caregivers
- The relationships between patient and caregiver accounts that you create by sharing those codes

### Device & Usage Information
- Push notification tokens, used to deliver medication reminders and caregiver alerts
- Your operating system (iOS or Android) and basic device info
- Anonymous crash reports and performance diagnostics, used only to identify and fix bugs
- Anonymous product interaction data (for example, which screens you use) to help us improve the app

We do **not** collect your location, contacts, browsing history, search history, or advertising identifiers. We do not access data from any other apps on your device.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Everything we collect exists to make the app work for you. Specifically, we use your information to:

- Track your medications and send you reminders to take them
- Calculate adherence statistics and surface useful insights like streaks and weekly progress
- Enable caregiver monitoring — sharing medication and adherence data with the specific caregivers you've explicitly linked via care code
- Notify caregivers when relevant events occur, like missed doses
- Warn you when your supply is running low so you can refill on time
- Identify and fix bugs through anonymous crash and performance data

We do **not** use your information for advertising, profiling, behavioral tracking, or any commercial purpose beyond providing the app itself.

---

## 3. How Your Information Is Shared

### With Caregivers You Authorize
When you share your unique care code with a caregiver, they can see your medications, dose history, and related health information within the app. This is the core of how PillBridge works, and it only happens when you explicitly share your code. You can revoke access at any time — either by asking the caregiver to stop monitoring you, or by generating a new care code that invalidates the old one.

### With Service Providers
PillBridge runs on Google Firebase, which provides our authentication, database, file storage, and cloud function services. Your data is stored on Google's secure servers and handled according to Google's privacy and security standards. Firebase is the only third-party service that processes your information.

We do not sell, rent, share, or license your data to advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

### Legal Requirements
If we're legally required to disclose information — by subpoena, court order, or valid legal process — we will comply, but we'll notify you first whenever the law permits.

---

## 4. How We Protect Your Data

- All data in transit is encrypted using industry-standard TLS
- All data at rest is encrypted on Google's Firebase infrastructure
- Passwords are handled exclusively by Firebase Authentication and are never visible to us
- Firestore security rules ensure that only you and your linked caregivers can access your data — nobody else, not even us during normal operation
- Photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage with access controls that match your account

No system is perfectly secure, but we've built PillBridge using the same security standards trusted by major healthcare and financial apps.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

You always have full control over your information. You can:

- **View** everything we store about you by opening the app
- **Edit or delete** any medication, profile detail, photo, or care link at any time directly in the app
- **Delete your account** entirely by emailing us at the address below. We'll permanently remove your account, medications, dose history, photos, and care group links within 30 days.
- **Revoke caregiver access** at any time by removing them from your care group or generating a new care code
- **Disable notifications** through your phone's settings, without affecting anything else about your account
- **Export your data** by emailing us — we'll send your full record in a readable format

### California Residents
If you live in California, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of any sale of your data. **PillBridge does not sell personal data to anyone, ever.**

### European Residents
If you live in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

PillBridge is designed for adults managing their own medications or caregivers managing a loved one's care. The app is not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will promptly delete the account and all associated data.

---

## 7. Important Health Disclaimer

PillBridge is a self-management tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. The reminders, statistics, and information in the app are designed to help you stay organized — they are **not** a substitute for advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

Always consult your doctor or pharmacist about your medications. PillBridge is **not** a HIPAA-covered entity and should not be used as an official medical record-keeping system by healthcare providers.
