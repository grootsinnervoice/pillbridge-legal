# PillBridge Support

Having trouble with PillBridge? Questions, bugs, or feature requests?

**Email:** pillbridge.support@gmail.com

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

## Common Questions

**How do I add a caregiver?**
Share your Care Code (shown on your dashboard) with someone. They can enter it from their PillBridge account to start monitoring.

**Can I export my data?**
Data export is coming in a future update. In the meantime, email us and we'll help.

**How do I delete my account?**
Email us from the address on your account and we'll delete your account and all associated data.

**How do I change my medication schedule?**
Tap any medication on your dashboard to open its details, then tap "Edit Medication" to change the schedule, dosage, or reminders.

**Does PillBridge work offline?**
PillBridge needs an internet connection to sync with your caregivers and update your data. Reminders still fire even without internet.
